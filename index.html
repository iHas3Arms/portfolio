About Me


My name is James Anthony John Brennen, and I would like to thank you for reading this page. I was born from a Romanian mother in 2009 and grew up with herself, my father - a British man - my 12-years-older brother, Ryan, who had a different Irish father - he unfortunately passed away when my brother was very young - and my 15-years-older sister (who's father is Romanian). I grew up in a town called Altrincham in the Northern city of Manchester. My siblings and I are all connected by our mother, however that's only a genetic way. We are mainly connected by our love and liking of one another. I have always been encouraged to do the things I enjoy and to follow my dreams, but also that to achieve these goals I must put time, effort, sweat and tears into them. Growing up with this mindset, I have so far lived a happy life, learning many skills along the way. I have developed great friendships, and have had them taken away. I have had people close to me taken away. When I was 7 years old, my aunt from my mother's side, Zina(Romanian name), was killed by cancer, just like both of my grandmothers. Through experiences like these, I have turned into a strong person who can deal with bad situations. However, not all of the bad experiences I've had are truly "bad". In fact, a lot of my "bad" experiences are merely inconvenient situations, however being in these situations so many times has given me a great sense of being adaptable and being able to throw solutions at problems when they come. A great example of this is my love of programming. I am a self-taught programmer, and I know multiple coding/programming languages, including JavaScript, HTML, or Hyper Text Markup Language, CSS, or Cascading Style Sheets, and C++. In fact, I coded this website myself with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Being a coder/programmer, I am forced to solve issues through the power of my mind, as I have to first figure out what is wrong, and then come up with a solution. Throughout this portfolio, there will be different sections, each containing information about myself or the things I do. Part of this is about my interest in programming, and includes projects I have made along the way, like a version of the infamous Google Dinosaur Game, a recreation of the popular Snake Game, and more. I enjoy working with others, as I like helping people when they need it, and I love sharpening my skills. However, I am not limited to this. I can work on my own just as well, however in any situation, it is usually preferable to work with another person, as if one makes a mistake, the other can help. I enjoy learning and can adapt to new environments with ease. I can learn new skills rather quickly as I am and always have been a fast-learner.
